# Wireless Audio Transmission via Laser

## 📌 Title

Wireless Audio Transmission via Laser

A wireless communication system that transmits audio signals through a laser beam and reproduces the sound at the receiver end using a photodetector and audio amplifier. This project demonstrates the principles of optical communication and wireless signal transmission using light.

## ⚙️ The Process

### 1. Transmitter Design
A microphone captures the audio signal, which is amplified and used to modulate the intensity of a laser beam.

### 2. Optical Transmission
The laser beam carries the audio information through free space without any wired connection.

### 3. Receiver Design
A solar panel acts as the photodetector and receives variations in laser intensity.

### 4. Signal Recovery
The received optical signal is converted back into an electrical audio signal.

### 5. Audio Amplification
An amplifier boosts the recovered signal to drive a speaker.

### 6. Testing and Optimization
The transmitter and receiver are aligned carefully to maximize signal strength and audio clarity.

## ▶️ Running the Project

### Components Required

- Laser Diode Module
- Solar Panel / Photodetector
- Microphone Module
- Audio Amplifier Module
- Speaker
- 9V Battery
- Breadboard
- Jumper Wires

### Steps

1. Assemble the transmitter circuit.
2. Connect the microphone to the laser modulation circuit.
3. Assemble the receiver circuit with the solar panel and amplifier.
4. Align the laser beam directly onto the solar panel.
5. Power both circuits.
6. Speak or play audio into the microphone.
7. Listen to the received audio through the speaker.

### Expected Output

The audio transmitted through the laser beam should be reproduced by the speaker at the receiver end with minimal delay.
